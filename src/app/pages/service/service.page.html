Labor Only
 Active Inactive

Description: Customers may request labor only assistance to arrive at a residence or business to assist with moving items. Tasks performed can include: Unloading / Loading, Relocating Furniture, dissembling items for moving, etc…. Helpers should be prepared to lift heavy weight as requested by customer.

Requirements: Government Issued Photo ID. Ability to travel to customer’s residence.
Item Delivery
 Active Inactive

Description: Customer will capture a picture / video with their mobile device and provide a brief description of the item. You as the service provider will have the ability to review the customer request and send a response with the amount to move an item. Consider time of travel, type of item, special equipment that may be required. Customer will select either 1 helper (just you and your vehicle) or 2 helpers (you, someone else and your vehicle).

Requirements: Government Issued Photo ID. Ability to travel to customer’s residence.
Junk Removal
 Active Inactive

Description: Customer will select this option when they require junk removal. We ask the customer to provide a description of what items will require moving. Customer should provide estimated weight, size, specific area, type of item, etc. Items could include: Construction debris such as wood, drywall, concrete, yard clippings, major appliance, bulky furniture, mattress, etc, tires, concrete removal, gas powered items.

Requirements: Government Issue Photo ID / Business License.

Full Move
 Active Inactive

Description: Customer will select this option when performing Larger moves typically Large Homes or Businesses. The work order will contain details such as: Service Date / Time of Day, stair wells inside and outside home, upper level / lower level square footage, total bedrooms, bathrooms, rooms upstairs, rooms downstairs, attic sq footage, patio, gas, etc… Utilize the message system to clarify any questionable service details prior to bidding.

Requirements: Government Issued Photo ID / Business License
